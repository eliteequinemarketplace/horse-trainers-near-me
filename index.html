Finding the Right Hunter Jumper Trainer

The right trainer is the single most important factor in a rider's development and show career. When evaluating a hunter jumper trainer, key considerations include their own competition background and credentials, their track record with riders at your level and goals, the quality of their program's horses and facilities, their communication style and approach to teaching, the culture of their barn community, and the practical logistics of location, cost, and show schedule.
